Our Story


We began as a humble garage band that only used play cover songs for well known bands.
We mainly performed during school parties or school events.
We finally got our big break when we decided to write and record an original song.
To market the song we asked some of our film study peers to record a music video for us.
After the video was uploaded it was noticed by a local record label and we decided to sign on.
It was only a matter of time before we became a hit band and we started doing soundtracks for movies.
We moved to a larger record label and that was that.
We still do some cover songs and perform at party events.
